A brief intro

I've been working in the web industry for over a decade, originally around the time when digital diplomacy saw the FCO better engage than ever before. With Martha Lane Fox's 2010 report, a new 'digital by default' era was ushered in, with expensive departmental sites making way for the one-size-fits-all GOV.UK platform, spearheaded by the creation of GDS and an influx of technical talent. It was an exciting time to be in government, but fortune took me into the start-up in Tokyo, Japan no less..

Feel free to say hello!
